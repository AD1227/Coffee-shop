About Us

Welcome to Café De Lumière - where every cup tells a story. Founded in 2012 in a quiet corner of the city, our café was inspired by the charm of European coffee houses and a passion for meaningful conversation over a perfect brew.

What began as a small neighborhood spot has grown into a beloved local retreat, known for its artisan coffee, warm atmosphere, and carefully curated pastries.

Whether you're here to work, relax, or reconnect, we're honored to be part of your everyday moments.
